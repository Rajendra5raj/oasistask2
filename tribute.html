Virat Kohli

Captain of the Indian Cricket Team

I adore Virat Kohli for his dedication and passion for cricket. He is one of the best batsmen in the world and has achieved numerous records in his career. He is also known for his aggressive style of play and leadership skills.

Virat Kohli was born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India. He made his debut for the Indian Cricket Team in 2008 and has been a regular member ever since. He was appointed as the captain of the team in 2017 and has led the team to many victories.

Some of the records held by Virat Kohli include the fastest batsman to score 8,000, 9,000, 10,000, and 11,000 runs in One Day Internationals. He also holds the record for the fastest century by an Indian batsman in a One Day International.

Virat Kohli is not only a great cricketer but also a role model for millions of people around the world. He is known for his fitness and discipline and has inspired many to lead a healthy lifestyle. He also supports various charitable causes and has donated to many organizations.
